Mobile banking


Our state of the Art mobile banking gives you the freedom to do more with your phone, you can choose using the USSD code or the mobile banking Application for smartphones and other Gadgets and have access to your bank 24/7 without any hustle.

Features of Mobile Banking (USSD and App)

  • Easy to use by anyone and comes in 3 languages (Kinyarwanda, English and French).
  • It works on any type of mobile device with no further configurations required
  • No registration fees and monthly fees
  • It is very safe

Requirements

  • Have an active BPR account.
  • Having a mobile phone connected to a network (MTN, Tigo).
  • Fill a mobile banking request form at any BPR branch(USSD)
  • Have a smart phone with data.
  • Download the App from Google play(Androids) and App stores(IOS)

What you can do with your mobile banking:

  • Balance inquiry.
  • Mini-statement.
  • Inter-Accounts transfer.
  • Transfer funds to BPR account.
  • Transfer to funds to izi cash and withdraw on BPR ATMs.
  • Transfer to other bank’s accounts instantly.
  • Transfer to Tigo cash
  • Pay for EWSA prepaid electricity.
  • Pay for star times TV.
  • Pay for Rwanda revenue taxes.
  • Pay for WASAC water Bills.
  • Order for a Cheque book.
