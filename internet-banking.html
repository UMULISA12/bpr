Internet banking


Our state of the Art mobile banking gives you the freedom to do more with your phone, you can choose using the USSD code or the mobile banking Application for smartphones and other Gadgets and have access to your bank 24/7 without any hustle.

Features of Internet Banking

  • Having access to your account information.
  • View and download bank account statements.
  • Airtime purchase(MTN and TIGO).
  • Electricity purchase.
  • View Exchange Rates daily.
  • Funds transfer( to bpr account and to other bank account).
  • Standing order (schedule payment).
  • Bulk payment such as Salaries.
  • RRA tax payment.
  • Banking tools requests i.e. Cheque book, receipt book and payment orders.

Requirements

  • Have an active BPR account.
  • Have access to the internet.
